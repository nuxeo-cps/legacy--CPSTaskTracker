Below are the dependencies depending on the version of CPS you are using :

CPS2 family :

 - Zope-2.6.1 or greater
 - CPS-2.1 or greater.
 - BTreeFolder2-0.5.0 or greater
 - CMFCalendar (for skins)
 - NuxCPSFlexibleDocument (version 1)

CPS3 family :

 - Zope-2.6.1 or greater (except 2.6.3)
 - CPS3.0 or greater
 - CPSSchemas / CPSDocuments
 - CPSNavigation
 - CPSInstaller
